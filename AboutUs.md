Guerrero Performance "Train like a Warrior"
<br>Founded by Octavio Guerrero in 2026, Guerrero Performance embodies the attitude of a warrior through consistent and informed training. Our mission is to support the community with consistent movement, pain reduction, and athletic skill acquisition.
<br>Octavio earned his B.S. in Kinesiology at Sacramento State University where he studied Kinesiology, lead a club for students/community, and taught fall prevention to the elderly. While expecting his first child, he became NASM certified and worked as a lead trainer in a high-performance gym where he eventually became General Manager. Octavio remains committed to continuing his education through NASM recerts,  NPE conferences, neurology courses, etc.
<br>Combining his 10 years of experience training ages 14-99 with his vast knowledge and a desire and dedication to helping others, Octavio has created a space that is welcoming and life-giving.
